My thanks go out to the authors of all these excellent products - without them Moodle would be missing important functionality.  Copyright information for this software is included below:

Copyright © 2000, 2001 John Lim (jlim@natsoft.com.my)
License: Dual LGPL and BSD-style

Class to draw line, point, bar, and area graphs, including numeric x-axis and double y-axis.
