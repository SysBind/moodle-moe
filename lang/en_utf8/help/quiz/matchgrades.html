Match Grades

Imported grades must match one of the fixed list of valid grades, as follows...

  • 100%
  • 90%
  • 80%
  • 75%
  • 70%
  • 66.666%
  • 60%
  • 50%
  • 40%
  • 33.333
  • 30%
  • 25%
  • 20%
  • 16.666%
  • 14.2857
  • 12.5%
  • 11.111%
  • 10%
  • 5%
  • 0%

negative values of the above list are also permitted
